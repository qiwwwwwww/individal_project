About

With the spreading usage of mobile devices, user generated data collection becomes a grey legal area issue. The reasons are due to less obvious position of usage statements and vague notifications by developers. In other words, there is a gap between users and developers on mobile data collections.

Pay by data presents one possible solutions to solve this situation, which is to introduce a new form of app store. This websites allows developers to upload their apk files and relevant details. Those informations will allow developers to collect users data in details, as well as limit further collection by developers once the they have reached their frequency.
